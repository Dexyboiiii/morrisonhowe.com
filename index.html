Hello, World!

Born in Lewisham, raised in Deal, and coming to a city near you! (as long as you're in Portsmouth)
I'm a Software Engineer currently working as a Junior Java Developer for Novatech. In 2023 I completed my BSc (Hons) at the University of Portsmouth, achieving a 2:1.

Previous to Novatech, I did a 14-month industrial placement for IBM UK Labs in Hursley. In my four years programming, I've worked with the IBM CICS Transaction Server, Java, Swing, Spring, Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, MSSQL/PostgreSQL, JavaScript, React, HTML/CSS, Dart, Flutter, Python, Bash, Docker and Kubernetes.

When I'm not glued to a keyboard, you can occasionally find me riding trails and taking photos.

Final Year Project - Podcast Scrobbler

PodcastScrobbler - an Android podcast client written in Kotlin with Jetpack Compose. It had the aim of parsing tracklists out of descriptions in music podcasts, approximating the currently playing track, and showing displaying it to the user. Although I was not able to complete it in time for the dissertation deadline, the end goal is for metadata to be sent to the music tracking service, Last.fm.
