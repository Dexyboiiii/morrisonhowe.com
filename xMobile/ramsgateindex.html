About Us

Ramsgate Mobiles is an electronic repair shop based in the beautiful harbour town of Ramsgate, Kent. We welcome anyone and provide consultation about issues to most electrical devices. During our opening hours you're welcome to give us a call; we are happy to help. We provide high quality repairs to all models pf phones, tablets, iPads, laptops and PCs. Our staff have long term experience, making the wait short whilst we carry out professional work on your device.

Mobile phone repairs(screens, battery, charging and others) on all models: Apple iPhone, Samsung, LG, Sony, Huawei, BlackBerry, Nokia, HTC and others

PC services: Unlocking, installing software, security screens, backup on more. Available on all types: Toshiba, Dell, Samsung, Acer, Asus, Lenovo, Apple Macs and others.

Most repairs take up to 30 minutes. Please feel free to call and ask us for a quote
